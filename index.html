Welcome to Foolish Faith Empowerment, where we advocate for personal growth through mindful decision-making aligned with your beliefs and values.



In this 90-day life coaching session, we're leveraging the concepts of foolishness and faithfulness:
  • "Foolish" typically refers to actions or decisions lacking wisdom or judgment, often involving reckless behavior.
  • On the flip side, "faith" embodies trust and conviction in a particular belief without the need for proof or evidence.
While learning, we all inevitably do something foolish. If we are going to be living foolishly, let's do it under our own terms and through our faith.
